Heart Disease Predictor

This prediction only for a school project to fulfill the requirement of Demystifying Machine Learning. We are not medical professionals. This application we use Python Pandas, HTML/CSS/Bootstrap and Heroku for hosting the application. The dataset has over 1000 records from a combination of 5 heart disease independently available datasets (Cleveland, Long Beach VA, Switzerland, Hungary, and Statlog). It does not intent to diagnose any Disease. Thank you for visiting our website.
